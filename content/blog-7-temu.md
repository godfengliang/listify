## Introduction

Temu is the fastest-growing marketplace in the US, with $30B+ in annual sales. But most sellers treat it like Amazon — and that's why they fail.

Temu buyers are **value-driven impulse shoppers.** Your listing needs to scream "great deal" in 5 seconds flat.

## Temu Listing Format

### Title Rules
- Keep it short (under 80 characters)
- Lead with the product type
- Highlight the #1 selling point
- Use simple, clear language

**Examples:**
- ✅ "Bluetooth Earbuds Wireless - 40H Battery, ANC, IPX7"
- ❌ "Premium High Quality Wireless Bluetooth 5.3 Earphones Earbuds TWS with Active Noise Cancellation for iPhone Android"

### Description Format

Use this template:

```
✅ [Product Name] — [Main Benefit]

[2-3 sentences about what makes it great]

PACKAGE INCLUDES:
• 1x [Product]
• 1x [Accessory]
• 1x [Accessory]

SPECIFICATIONS:
• [Spec 1]
• [Spec 2]
• [Spec 3]

⭐ [Number sold] sold | ⭐ [Rating] stars
```

### Selling Points (3-5 bullets)
Focus on value, not technical specs:

1. "40-hour battery = charge once per week"
2. "IPX7 waterproof = use anywhere"
3. "ANC = block noise for $30, not $300"

## Temu Pricing Strategy

- Price 30-50% below Amazon equivalent
- Show original price vs sale price
- Use bundle deals (buy 2 get 10% off)
- Flash sales for new product launches

## Common Temu Listing Mistakes

1. **Too technical** — Temu shoppers don't care about Bluetooth version numbers
2. **Too long** — If they can't read it in 10 seconds, they'll scroll past
3. **No trust signals** — Add sold count and star rating
4. **Copied from Amazon** — Amazon-style listings don't work on Temu
