## Introduction

Your Amazon bullet points are the #1 conversion driver on your listing page. After the main image, shoppers read the bullets before anything else.

Yet most sellers write boring, feature-focused bullets that put people to sleep.

Here are 5 proven bullet point formulas with templates you can copy.

## Formula 1: Problem → Solution

**Template:**
> [ALL CAPS HOOK] — [Problem] → [How this product solves it]

**Example:**
> 🎧 NEVER STRUGGLE WITH TANGLED WIRES AGAIN — Our wireless earbuds eliminate the frustration of knotted cords. Bluetooth 5.3 connection is instant and stable, so your music starts the moment you put them on.

## Formula 2: Number + Benefit

**Template:**
> [NUMBER] [UNIT] OF [BENEFIT] — [What this means for the customer]

**Example:**
> 🔋 40 HOURS OF BATTERY LIFE — That's a full week of commuting, working out, and relaxing without reaching for a charger. Just 10 minutes of charging gives you 3 more hours.

## Formula 3: Comparison + Value

**Template:**
> SAME [QUALITY] AS [EXPENSIVE BRAND] — [What you get for less]

**Example:**
> 🔇 ACTIVE NOISE CANCELLATION ON PAR WITH $200+ EARBUDS — Block out airplane noise, office chatter, and gym grunts. Premium ANC technology at a fraction of the cost.

## Formula 4: Use Case + Emotion

**Template:**
> PERFECT FOR [SITUATION] — [Emotional benefit]

**Example:**
> 🏃 BUILT FOR YOUR TOUGHEST WORKOUTS — IPX7 waterproof means sweat, rain, and even accidental washing machine trips won't stop your music. Push harder, run longer, never worry.

## Formula 5: Technical Spec → Real Benefit

**Template:**
> [SPEC] [FEATURE] — [Why normal people should care]

**Example:**
> ⚡ BLUETOOTH 5.3 CHIP — The latest wireless technology means zero audio lag during videos and games, plus 50% better battery efficiency than older Bluetooth versions.

## Quick Reference Cheat Sheet

| Bullet Position | Best Formula | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Bullet 1 | Problem → Solution | Hook attention |
| Bullet 2 | Number + Benefit | Prove value |
| Bullet 3 | Comparison | Show savings |
| Bullet 4 | Use Case | Create desire |
| Bullet 5 | Technical Spec | Build trust |
