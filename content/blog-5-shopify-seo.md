## The 5-Part Shopify Product Description Formula

### Part 1: The Hook (1-2 sentences)
Start with the customer's pain point or desire.

> "Tired of earbuds that die halfway through your workout?"

### Part 2: The Solution (2-3 sentences)
Introduce your product as the answer.

> "SoundWave Pro earbuds deliver 40 hours of battery with active noise cancelling — so your music never stops."

### Part 3: Key Benefits (5 bullets)
Focus on outcomes, not features.

> - 🎧 **40-hour battery** — Listen all week without charging
> - 🏃 **IPX7 waterproof** — Sweat through any workout
> - 🔇 **Active Noise Cancelling** — Block out the world
> - 📱 **Bluetooth 5.3** — Instant, stable connection
> - ✋ **Touch controls** — Skip tracks without reaching for your phone

### Part 4: Social Proof (1-2 sentences)
> "Join 50,000+ happy customers. Rated 4.8/5 stars."

### Part 5: The CTA (1 sentence)
> "Order now and get free 2-day shipping."

## FAQ

### How long should a Shopify product description be?
300-1000 words. Long enough for SEO, short enough to keep attention.

### Should I use HTML in descriptions?
Yes. Use `<h2>`, `<h3>`, `<p>`, `<ul>`, `<li>` tags. Shopify renders these in your theme.

### Can I use the same description on Amazon?
No. Amazon and Shopify have different algorithms. Amazon needs keyword-dense bullets. Shopify needs storytelling + SEO.
