## Introduction

TikTok Shop is the fastest-growing e-commerce platform in 2026. But selling here is completely different from Amazon or Shopify.

On TikTok, **the video sells the product.** Your listing just needs to support the video's claims and make checkout frictionless.

Here's how to write TikTok Shop listings that convert viewers into buyers.

## TikTok Shop Listing Anatomy

### 1. Title (Short & Punchy)
- Max 60 characters
- Use emojis (1-2 max)
- Include the "wow factor"

**Examples:**
- ✅ "🔥 SoundWave Pro — 40H Battery Earbuds"
- ❌ "Wireless Bluetooth Earbuds with Active Noise Cancelling Technology IPX7 Waterproof"

### 2. Product Description (Conversational)
Write like you're texting a friend:

> "Okay so these earbuds are actually insane 😳
> 40 HOURS of battery. That's a whole week without charging.
> The noise cancelling? Chef's kiss. Perfect for the gym, commute, anywhere.
> Touch controls, waterproof, the whole package.
> 50K+ people already got them. Your turn 👇"

### 3. Price Display
- Show original price crossed out
- Display discount percentage
- Create urgency ("Flash sale ends in 2 hours")

### 4. Video Script (15-30 seconds)

**The Formula:**
- **Hook (0-2 sec):** "You NEED these earbuds"
- **Problem (2-5 sec):** Show terrible wired earbuds tangling
- **Solution (5-15 sec):** Demo the product with music playing
- **Social proof (15-20 sec):** "50K sold and counting"
- **CTA (20-25 sec):** "Link below, grab yours before they sell out"

## TikTok Shop vs Amazon Listing Differences

| Element | Amazon | TikTok Shop |
|---|---|---|
| Tone | Professional, SEO-focused | Casual, trendy, emoji-friendly |
| Title length | 200 chars | 60 chars |
| Keywords | Dense and varied | Minimal, hashtag-driven |
| Main selling tool | Listing itself | Video + listing |
| Buyer mindset | Researching purchase | Impulse buy |
