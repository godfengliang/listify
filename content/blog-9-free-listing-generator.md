## What Makes a "Professional" Amazon Listing?

1. **Keyword-optimized title** — Under 200 characters, includes primary + secondary keywords
2. **5 benefit-driven bullet points** — Each starts with a hook, explains the benefit
3. **Detailed description** — 1000-2000 characters, natural keyword integration
4. **Backend search terms** — Up to 250 bytes of high-value keywords
5. **A+ Content** — Enhanced brand content (optional but powerful)

A good listing can increase conversion rate by 30-50%. That's the difference between losing money and being profitable.
