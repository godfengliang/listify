### The Problem
Cross-border e-commerce sellers waste 2-3 hours PER PRODUCT writing different listings for Amazon, Shopify, Temu, and TikTok Shop. Each platform has different rules, character limits, and SEO requirements.

### The Solution
**Listify** uses AI to generate optimized listings for ALL platforms in one click:
- 📦 **Amazon** — Title, 5 Bullet Points, Description, Search Terms
- 🛍️ **Shopify** — SEO Title, Description, Meta Tags, URL Handle
- 🔥 **Temu** — Platform-optimized title, description, selling points
- 🎵 **TikTok Shop** — Short copy + video script (Hook/Body/CTA)
- 🔍 **Bonus** — SEO keywords & hashtags for all platforms
