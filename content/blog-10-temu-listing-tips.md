## Introduction

Temu's algorithm is different from Amazon's. It rewards:
- **Value perception** (not brand prestige)
- **Clear, scannable content** (not long descriptions)
- **High engagement** (clicks, saves, purchases)

After analyzing 500+ top-performing Temu listings, these 5 patterns show up again and again.

## Tip 1: Front-Load Your Best Selling Point

Temu shoppers scroll fast. Your title needs to sell in 2 seconds.

**Bad:** "Premium Quality Wireless Earbuds with Multiple Features"
**Good:** "40H Battery Earbuds — ANC, IPX7, Only $24.99"

The price, battery life, and key features are all visible immediately.

## Tip 2: Use the "Problem → Solution" Description Format

Temu buyers respond to pain points:

> **Problem:** Tangled wires, dead batteries, terrible sound
> **Solution:** Wireless earbuds with 40H battery, crystal clear audio, and IPX7 waterproof protection

This format works because Temu shoppers are actively comparing options and need to be convinced quickly.

## Tip 3: Include Comparison Value (Most Sellers Ignore This)

Show what they'd pay elsewhere:

> "Same ANC quality as $200 earbuds — at 1/8 the price"
> "Comparable to AirPods Pro features for just $24.99"

This triggers the "too good to pass up" response that drives impulse purchases on Temu.

## Tip 4: Bullet Points > Paragraphs

Temu's mobile-first layout means paragraphs get skipped. Use short bullets:

- ✅ 40 hours battery life
- ✅ Active noise cancellation
- ✅ Waterproof IPX7
- ✅ Touch controls
- ✅ Type-C fast charging

## Tip 5: Add Social Proof Numbers

> "10,000+ sold | 4.8 stars"

Even if the product is new, you can use early numbers. Temu shoppers trust quantity.
