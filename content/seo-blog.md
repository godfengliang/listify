## Platform-by-Platform Listing Requirements

### Amazon Listing Best Practices
- **Title**: Under 200 characters. Format: Brand + Core Feature + Keywords. Don't keyword-stuff.
- **Bullet Points**: 5 points, each starting with a capital letter. Focus on benefits, not features.
- **Description**: 1000-2000 characters. Include primary keywords naturally.
- **Backend Search Terms**: Max 250 bytes. Never repeat words from the title.
- **Pro tip**: Amazon's A9 algorithm prioritizes conversion rate. Write for humans first, keywords second.

### Shopify Product Page Optimization
- **SEO Title**: Under 60 characters for Google. Include primary keyword.
- **Description**: 500-1000 words, HTML formatted. Tell a story.
- **Meta Description**: Under 155 characters. This is your ad copy in Google search results.
- **URL Handle**: Short, lowercase, hyphenated. E.g., `wireless-bluetooth-earbuds-anc`
- **Pro tip**: Shopify pages can rank on Google. Optimize for organic search, not just conversion.

### Temu Listing Format
- **Title**: Short and punchy. Emphasize price-value ratio.
- **Description**: Simple and direct. Bullet points of key features.
- **Selling Points**: 3-5 points max. Focus on what makes it a great deal.
- **Pro tip**: Temu shoppers buy on impulse. Make your listing scannable in 5 seconds.

### TikTok Shop Listing Strategy
- **Title**: Eye-catching, scroll-stopping. Emoji-friendly.
- **Description**: Conversational, energetic. Like talking to a friend.
- **Video Script**: 15-30 seconds. Hook in 2 seconds, demo the product, clear CTA.
- **Pro tip**: The video sells. Your listing just needs to support the video's claims.
