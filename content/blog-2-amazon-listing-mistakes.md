## Introduction

Your Amazon listing is your storefront. If it's poorly optimized, you're losing sales every single day — even if your product is great.

After analyzing thousands of Amazon listings, these are the 7 most common mistakes that kill conversion rates and search rankings.

## Mistake 1: Keyword-Stuffed Titles

**Bad:** "Wireless Earbuds Bluetooth Earbuds TWS Earbuds Noise Cancelling Earbuds Waterproof Earbuds"

**Good:** "SoundWave Pro Wireless Earbuds — 40H Battery, Active Noise Cancelling, IPX7 Waterproof, Bluetooth 5.3"

**Why:** Amazon's A9 algorithm penalizes keyword stuffing. Write for humans, optimize for keywords naturally.

## Mistake 2: Feature-Focused Bullets (Not Benefit-Focused)

**Bad:** "Bluetooth 5.3 technology with 40-hour battery"

**Good:** "🎧 NEVER RUN OUT OF BATTERY — 40 hours of playtime means you can listen all week without reaching for a charger"

**Why:** Customers don't buy features. They buy outcomes. Every bullet should answer "what's in it for me?"

## Mistake 3: Ignoring Backend Search Terms

Your backend search terms have a 250-byte limit. Most sellers either:
- Leave them empty (losing 30% of potential keywords)
- Repeat words from the title (wasting space)
- Use competitor brand names (against Amazon policy)

**Fix:** Use unique, high-volume keywords that don't appear in your title or bullets.

## Mistake 4: No A/B Testing

The difference between a 10% conversion rate and 15% conversion rate can mean $50K+ per year in additional revenue.

**What to test:**
- Main image (white background vs. lifestyle)
- Title format (brand first vs. keyword first)
- Bullet point order (most important benefit first)

## Mistake 5: Missing Long-Tail Keywords

"Wireless earbuds" has 500K monthly searches but insane competition.

"Noise cancelling wireless earbuds for running" has 5K searches but almost no competition. Convert at 3x higher rate.

**Fix:** Target 10-20 specific long-tail keywords across your listing.

## Mistake 6: Weak Product Description

Your description should:
- Paint a picture of the customer using your product
- Address common objections
- Include social proof ("Join 50,000+ happy customers")
- Reinforce key benefits from bullets

## Mistake 7: Same Listing Across Platforms

Your Amazon listing won't work on Shopify. Your Temu listing shouldn't be copy-pasted to TikTok Shop. Each platform has different:
- Character limits
- Algorithm preferences
- Buyer psychology
- SEO requirements
