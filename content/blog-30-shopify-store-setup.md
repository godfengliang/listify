## Step 1: Choose Your Plan

## Step 2: Pick a Domain Name

**Option A: Buy through Shopify** ($10-15/year)
- Easiest: auto-configured DNS
- Managed in Shopify dashboard

**Option B: Buy elsewhere** (Namecheap, GoDaddy, Google Domains)
- Cheaper (sometimes $5-8/year)
- Then connect to Shopify manually

**Domain naming rules:**
- Keep it under 15 characters
- No hyphens, no numbers (people forget them)
- `.com` if available, otherwise `.co` or `.shop`
- Match your brand name

✅ `soundwavepro.com`
❌ `sound-wave-pro-earbuds-2026.shop`

## Step 4: Customize Your Store

Navigate to Online Store → Customize. Here's what to set up:

### Header
- Upload your logo (PNG with transparent background)
- Logo size: 200-300px wide
- Menu: Home / Shop / About / Contact

### Homepage Sections

**Must-have sections (in order):**

1. **Hero banner** — Your best product image + headline + CTA button
2. **Featured products** — 4-8 top products
3. **Collection list** — Category links (e.g., Earbuds, Chargers, Accessories)
4. **About snippet** — 2-3 sentences about your brand
5. **Email signup** — Capture emails for marketing
6. **Footer** — Contact, policies, social links

### Product Page Checklist

Each product needs:
- [ ] Title (brand + product name + key feature)
- [ ] Price (set to compare-at-price for discounted look)
- [ ] 5-7 product images (main + lifestyle + details)
- [ ] Description (benefits, not just features)
- [ ] Variants (size, color, etc.)
- [ ] SKU (for inventory tracking)
- [ ] Search engine listing (meta title + description)

## Step 5: Set Up Payments

Settings → Payments → Activate Shopify Payments

**Accept these payment methods:**
- ✅ Visa / Mastercard / Amex
- ✅ Apple Pay / Google Pay
- ✅ PayPal
- ✅ Shop Pay (Shopify's own — higher conversion rate)

**Fee note:** Using Shopify Payments avoids the 2% transaction fee Shopify charges when you use third-party payment gateways.

---

## Step 6: Configure Shipping

Settings → Shipping and Delivery

### Domestic Shipping Rates

| Strategy | When to Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Free shipping (recommended) | Products $25+ with decent margins | Free on all orders |
| Free over $X | Lower-priced products | Free over $35 |
| Flat rate | Products with consistent shipping cost | $4.99 flat rate |
| Calculated | Heavy/oversized products | Real-time carrier rates |

**Recommendation:** Free shipping. Products with free shipping convert 20-30% better. Just build the shipping cost into your product price.

### International Shipping

Start with 1-3 countries (US + Canada + UK is a good starting set). Expand as you learn.

## Step 7: Set Up Legal Pages

Create these pages (Settings → Policies):

1. **Privacy Policy** — Shopify generates a template, customize it
2. **Refund/Return Policy** — Clearly state your return window and conditions
3. **Terms of Service** — Shopify generates a template
4. **Shipping Policy** — Processing times, carriers, international info

Add all to footer menu.

## Step 9: Add Google Analytics + Search Console

### Google Analytics (GA4)

1. Create a GA4 property at analytics.google.com
2. Copy the Measurement ID (G-XXXXXXXXXX)
3. Shopify → Online Store → Preferences → Google Analytics → Paste ID

### Google Search Console

1. Go to search.google.com/search-console
2. Add property → URL prefix → enter your domain
3. Copy the TXT record from Google
4. Shopify → Settings → Domains → Manage → Add DNS record
5. Verify in Search Console

This is crucial for SEO. Do it on day 1.

## FAQ

### Shopify vs Amazon — which should I start first?
Start with Amazon if you want immediate access to an audience (but higher fees and competition). Start with Shopify if you want to build a brand (but you're responsible for traffic). Many successful sellers do both.

### How long does it take to make the first sale on Shopify?
Without ads: 2-8 weeks (organic traffic takes time). With ads: potentially day 1, but profitability takes 2-4 weeks of optimization.

### Can I use Shopify if I'm outside the US?
Yes. Shopify is available in 175+ countries. Payments work with local payment gateways.

### Do I need a business license for Shopify?
In most countries, no — you can sell as an individual. But check your local regulations. For the US, you'll need an EIN or SSN for tax purposes once you start making significant revenue.
