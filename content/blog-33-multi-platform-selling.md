## Why Each Platform Needs a Different Listing

Before we talk about HOW, let's understand WHY you can't copy-paste:

| Element | Amazon | Shopify | Temu | TikTok Shop |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Title** | 200 chars, keyword-heavy | 60 chars, SEO-focused | Short, price-focused | Short, hook-driven |
| **Description** | 2000 chars, keyword-rich | Story-driven, brand voice | Brief, benefits-focused | Casual, conversational |
| **Keywords** | Backend search terms | Meta tags + page SEO | Built into title | Hashtags |
| **Images** | White background required | Any style, lifestyle | Bright, attention-grabbing | Video-first |
| **Price display** | Compare-at-price | Sale badges | "Almost sold out" urgency | Limited-time offers |
| **Buyer psychology** | Search → compare → buy | Browse → trust → buy | Scroll → impulse buy | Watch → want → buy |

**Copy-pasting an Amazon listing to TikTok Shop = 0 sales.** The buyer psychology is completely different.

## The 80/20 Approach to Multi-Platform Listings

You don't need to write everything from scratch. Here's the framework:

### Core Assets (Write Once)

1. **Product fact sheet** — all specs, features, materials, dimensions
2. **Benefit bank** — 10-15 customer benefits (not features)
3. **Keyword pool** — 20-30 keywords from research
4. **Objection list** — 5-10 reasons people might NOT buy (and your counterpoints)

These 4 assets power ALL your platform listings.

### Platform-Specific Layers (Adapt Per Platform)

| Layer | Amazon | Shopify | Temu | TikTok Shop |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Title formula** | Brand + Keyword + 3 Features | Keyword + Benefit + Brand | Hook + Price + Benefit | Hook + Emotion + CTA |
| **Tone** | Professional, detailed | Brand voice, story | Direct, price-conscious | Casual, fun, urgent |
| **Length** | Long (1500-2000 chars) | Medium (500-1000 words) | Short (200-500 chars) | Very short (100-300 chars) |
| **CTA** | "Add to Cart" | "Shop Now" | "Buy Now — Almost Gone" | "Link in Bio 🔥" |

## Common Multi-Platform Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Same Price Everywhere

TikTok Shop buyers are more price-sensitive. Amazon buyers compare prices. Shopify buyers value brand experience.

**Strategy:** 
- Amazon: Competitive market price
- Shopify: 10-15% higher (brand premium)
- Temu: Lowest price (acquire customers)
- TikTok Shop: Promotional price (limited-time offer)

### Mistake 2: Same Inventory Pool

Selling the same FBA inventory on Amazon and Shopify? What happens when you run out of stock on Amazon? You lose ranking. And Shopify orders can't be fulfilled.

**Strategy:** Separate inventory pools. Or use FBA Multi-Channel Fulfillment (FBA ships Shopify orders too).

### Mistake 3: Ignoring Platform-Specific Policies

- Amazon: Strict rules on images, claims, external links
- Shopify: You control everything (but no built-in traffic)
- Temu: They control pricing (you're a supplier)
- TikTok Shop: Content guidelines change frequently

**Read each platform's policies before you list.**

## FAQ

### Can I use FBA inventory for Shopify orders?
Yes. Amazon Multi-Channel Fulfillment lets FBA ship orders from Shopify, eBay, or your own website. Fees are higher than standard FBA but lower than renting your own warehouse.

### Do I need separate UPC codes for each platform?
No. One GS1 UPC/EAN works across all platforms. But each platform has its own product ID system (ASIN on Amazon, SKU on Shopify, etc.).

### Which platform should I start with if I can only pick one?
Amazon. It has the most buyers actively searching for products. Once you have traction on Amazon, add Shopify (own your brand) and TikTok Shop (reach new audiences).
