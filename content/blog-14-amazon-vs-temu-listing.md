## Introduction

Selling on both Amazon and Temu is smart. But using the same listing on both platforms is a mistake that will cost you sales.

Here are the 7 critical differences and how to handle each one.

## Difference 1: Title Length & Style

**Amazon:** Up to 200 characters. Keyword-rich. Format: Brand + Key Feature + Keywords
**Temu:** Under 80 characters. Direct and punchy. Lead with product type and top feature.

## Difference 2: Description Format

**Amazon:** Long-form (1000-2000 chars). Paragraphs with keyword integration. Professional tone.
**Temu:** Short and scannable. Bullet points and value-focused. Conversational tone.

## Difference 3: Keyword Strategy

**Amazon:** Dense keyword placement across title, bullets, description, and backend search terms.
**Temu:** Fewer keywords, more focused on product category and value proposition.

## Difference 4: Bullet Points vs Selling Points

**Amazon:** 5 detailed bullet points starting with ALL CAPS hooks. Benefit-driven.
**Temu:** 3-5 short selling points. Direct and simple.

## Difference 5: Pricing Positioning

**Amazon:** Value + quality. Justify higher prices with features and benefits.
**Temu:** Pure value. Emphasize price advantage and comparison to expensive alternatives.

## Difference 6: Social Proof

**Amazon:** Reviews build trust over time.
**Temu:** Sales volume and star rating matter most.

## Difference 7: Visual Content

**Amazon:** White background main image + lifestyle images + A+ content.
**Temu:** Multiple angles showing value. Price comparison visuals perform well.
